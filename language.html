ST. MARY'S SCHOOL
 Ashram Road, Mainpuri(U.P.)


SUBJECTS and LANGUAGES

Primary The students of classes I-III are taught English, Hindi, Mathematics and E.V.S. and the students of classes IV-V are taught Social Science in place of E.V.S. Computer Literacy Programmers has been adapted for all the Primary classes.

Secondary The students of classes VI-IX learn atleast one extra language in addition to Hindi, viz., English. The following are the subjects of study in this School for Classes IX-X:
  • Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English, Hindi, Social Studies & Health and Physical Education.
  • Work Exprience(Fine Arts, Eletronics, Music, Dance, Computer Science, Home Science).
Senior The subjects of study offered for classes XI and XII :
  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, English & Computer Science
  • Business Studies, Accounts & Political Science.
