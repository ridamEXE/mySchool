MOTTO

The purpose of this school motto is to provide a high standard of education, to equip students to cope with the challenges in day to day life and also to nurture young students to be a success in this fast changing competitive world. Besides this, to make them worthy citizens of India so that they are capable to shoulder intellectual, personal, ethical, social, and spiritual responsibilities.
