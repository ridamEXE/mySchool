Welcome To St. Mary's.....


St. Mary's School is a Co-educational, Non-Aided Christian Minority Institution which fosters academic distinction within the Christian tradition.The school has been growing in strength and achievements in all directions of educational endeavour from academics to sports, music and dance to computers. Above all there is a focus on value education and a renewed effort to pursue and enrich our basic moral values and cultural heritage coupled with a sense of social responsibility. The school has art building and other facilities to provide excellent shape to the future of your kids. The school is affiliated to CBSE board that offers education from pre-nursery to XII.
